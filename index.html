Services

Remote Dosimetry Planning Support

Remote planning capacity to help clinics manage staffing gaps, vacation coverage, and backlog. Work is performed by experienced dosimetrists operating within your facility’s planning standards.

  • IMRT / VMAT planning support (site-specific)
  • SBRT planning assistance (as requested)
  • Plan revisions, re-optimizations, and second-set planning support
  • Secure access via clinic-approved VPN/Citrix environments

Final clinical approvals remain with the facility’s credentialed providers per local policy.

Linac QA Program Support

Standardized monthly and annual QA documentation aligned with clinical operations.

  • Baseline and tolerance structure
  • Trend tracking and action thresholds
  • Report cleanup and standardization

Compliance & Audit Readiness
